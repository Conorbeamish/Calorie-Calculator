The Calorie Calculator

Whether you are looking how to lose weight or the best way to gain muscle mass, the answer comes down to a simple calculation to work out how many calories you need to consume each day. Calories in vs calories out.

We simply work out your Base Metabolic Rate (BMR) based on certain factors and compare this to your activity level to recommend suitable calorie intake for you.
